About Us

Welcome to SADAS! We are a team of passionate developers and designers committed to building an innovative platform that helps college students stay organized and manage their responsibilities more effectively. This website was developed as our capstone project for our Software Engineering course at Saint Xavier University, providing us with valuable experience in team collaboration and hands-on exposure to tools and methodologies commonly used in full-stack development.
