What Our Web App Offers

SADAS is a web platform designed to help college students efficiently track their daily tasks, including assignments, perosnal chores, budget management, and important reminders. Our goal is to provide students with an all-in-one solution to stay organized and manage their responsibilities with ease. Additionally, SADAS offers a subscription service that unlocks customization options and premium features, allowing users to tailor their experience to best fit their needs.

Task Tracking
Keep your tasks organized and never miss a deadline again. Our intuitive task tracking feature allows you to create, manage, and prioritize your assignments with ease. Set due dates, categorize your tasks, and receive timely reminders to stay on top of everything effortlessly.
Budget Management
Take control of your finances with our budget management tool. Track your expenses, set monthly budgets, and analyze spending habits with easy-to-read charts and reports. Whether you're saving for a goal or just trying to cut back on unnecessary expenses, we've got you covered.
Reminders & Alerts
Never forget an important event or deadline again. Our reminders and alerts system ensures you stay informed about upcoming tasks, meetings, and appointments. Customize notifications to fit your schedule and receive alerts via email, push notifications, or SMS.
Calendar Sync
Keep all your schedules in one place with our calendar sync feature. Seamlessly integrate with Google Calendar, Outlook, and other scheduling apps to manage your personal and professional life efficiently. Plan ahead with daily, weekly, and monthly views.
Customization
Make the app truly yours with our customization options. Adjust themes, layouts, and preferences to suit your workflow and style. Choose from a variety of color schemes, font sizes, and widget arrangements to create an experience that best fits your needs.
