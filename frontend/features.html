What We Offer

SADAS is a web platform designed to help college students efficiently track their daily tasks, including assignments, personal chores, budget management, and important reminders. Our goal is to provide students with an all-in-one solution to stay organized and manage their responsibilities with ease. Additionally, SADAS offers customization options, allowing users to tailor their experience to best fit their needs.

Task Tracking
Keep your tasks organized and never miss a deadline again. Our intuitive task tracking feature allows you to create, manage, and prioritize your assignments with ease. Set due dates, categorize your tasks, and receive timely reminders to stay on top of everything effortlessly.
Budget Management
Take control of your finances with our Budget Management tool. Easily track your income, expenses, and set budgets for different categories. Stay on top of your financial goals and never overspend again with helpful visualizations and real-time updates.
Reminders & Alerts
Stay on top of your schedule with our Reminders & Alerts feature. Get notified about important deadlines, meetings, and tasks. Customize your reminders to fit your needs and ensure that you never forget an important event again.
Calendar Sync
Seamlessly integrate your calendar with our app. Sync your events, appointments, and reminders across all your devices. Stay organized and manage your time efficiently with an up-to-date view of your schedule in one place.
Customization
Personalize your experience with our Customization feature. Change themes, layout, and settings to suit your preferences. Whether you need a more visual experience or a minimalistic approach, our customization options let you tailor the app to your needs.
Assignment Tracking
Keep track of your assignments with ease. Manage deadlines, progress, and important details. Stay organized and ensure that all your assignments are completed on time without the stress of missing anything.
Messaging
Stay connected with others via our integrated messaging feature. Send and receive messages in real-time, share important updates, and keep in touch with friends, colleagues, or clients without leaving the app.
Video Calling
Experience high-quality video calls with our built-in video calling feature. Connect with others for meetings, discussions, or casual conversations. Enjoy seamless communication with no interruptions and great clarity.
Social
Build connections with friends, family, and colleagues through our Social feature. Add friends, chat, and share updates seamlessly. Strengthen your network and enjoy a more connected experience within the app.
